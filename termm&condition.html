Mission:

To foster a culture of continuous improvement and excellence in the business community of the emirate of Sharjah, by recognizing and promoting outstanding achievements, innovation, and sustainable growth across all sectors.

Vision:

To be the beacon of excellence and innovation in the heart of the Middle East, positioning the emirate of Sharjah as a global hub where businesses and investments converge, setting the gold standard for performance excellence, and contributing to the sustainable development of the GCC region.

Objectives:

Recognize and Celebrate Excellence: The SHJSEEN Award seeks to recognize and celebrate exceptional accomplishments and innovations across the business landscape in Sharjah, honoring organizations that consistently strive for excellence.
Promote Continuous Improvement: We aim to encourage a culture of continuous improvement within organizations, inspiring them to adopt best practices, foster innovation, and deliver outstanding results to enhance the competitiveness of the emirate.
Enhance Competitiveness: Our objective is to elevate the competitiveness of Sharjah's businesses on the global stage by assisting them in achieving higher levels of performance excellence, which, in turn, will attract more investments and opportunities.
Foster Sustainability: We are committed to promoting sustainable business practices and social responsibility, encouraging organizations to integrate environmental, social, and governance (ESG) principles into their strategies and operations.
Inspire Innovation: The SHJSEEN Award aims to inspire and reward innovation in products, services, and processes, fostering a business environment that embraces change and creativity as a means to long-term success.
Cultivate Collaboration: We believe in fostering collaborations and partnerships among businesses, creating a network that shares knowledge, experiences, and resources to uplift the entire business community.
Contribute to Economic Growth: Our award endeavors to contribute to the economic growth and diversification of the emirate, attracting national and international investors who seek to engage with organizations committed to excellence.
Empower the Workforce: We strive to empower the workforce by encouraging organizations to invest in their employees' development and well-being, recognizing that a skilled, motivated, and engaged workforce is crucial for overall success.
Set Global Standards: SHJSEEN Award aims to set global benchmarks for performance excellence, influencing best practices and quality standards that are recognized and respected on an international scale.
Uphold Ethical Values: We are committed to upholding the highest ethical standards in business operations and conduct, promoting transparency, integrity, and responsible corporate governance.
